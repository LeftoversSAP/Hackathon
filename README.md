# Hackathon
# CivicConnect AI

## Problem Statement

Citizens often do not know which government department is responsible for resolving their civic issues. As a result, complaints are sent to the wrong department, causing delays and frustration.

Examples:

* Water leakage
* Potholes
* Broken streetlights
* Garbage collection issues
* Traffic signal problems

---

## Proposed Solution

CivicConnect AI is a web platform where citizens simply describe their issue in natural language.

The system automatically:

* Identifies the responsible department
* Generates a complaint draft
* Provides department details
---

## Existing Solution

Current complaint systems are fragmented across multiple portals and helplines.

Citizens must:

1. Identify the correct department.
2. Visit the respective portal.
3. Submit a complaint manually.

This process is confusing and time-consuming.

---

## What Makes Our Solution Unique?

* Single platform for all civic complaints
* AI-based department identification
* Automatic complaint format generation
* Reduced misdirected complaints

---

## Scope

**Focus Area:** Telangana (State Level)

Departments covered:

* Municipal Administration
* Water Board
* Electricity Board
* Traffic Police
* Roads & Buildings
* HMDA
